About Me

I'm Akhil Mulukutla, a UI/UX designer and creative technologist passionate about crafting intuitive digital experiences. Currently pursuing my Master's in Creative Technology at Southern Methodist University, I'm set to graduate in December 2025.

My journey in design began at the University of Texas at Dallas, where I earned my Bachelor's in Arts, Technology, and Emerging Communication in 2022. This foundation fueled my curiosity to blend creativity with cutting-edge technology.

What truly excites me is the potential of emerging technologies. From developing AI-powered interviewers to designing digital identity solutions like the Texas ID project, I'm always eager to push the boundaries of what's possible in UX design.
